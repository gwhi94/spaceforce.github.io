Welcome to Space Force!

Instructions to Play


Start the game by clicking the Start game button above

Player 1 will be able to go first, choose a tile up down left or right, both players will start with an evenly matched weapon

You can move up to 3 squares during one go, you cant move through walls

You can also pick weapons up that are scattered randomly around the map, by picking up a weapon, you will drop your current weapon

Once player one has finished, it is player 2's go. Player 2 can start their go by clicking Player 2 Go button above. Either player may end their go early by clicking the end go button.

If you meet the other player during the game, a fight will occur. The player who moved last will get to attack first, unless it is their last move.

Players will take turns in attacking, if you choose to defend then you will sustain 50% less damage from the next attack

The fight will end when one player is at 0 health, so ensure you pick up the best weapons!

After the game has ended, you will be able to reset the game and play again, this time player 2 will start.
